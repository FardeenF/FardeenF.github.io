Hi, I'm Fardeen Faisal!

I'm a passionate game developer from Ajax, Ontario.
I have experience with C++, Unity, OpenGL, Dart and more.
I'm an eager programmer who always looks for opportunities to grow!

Welcome! I'm Fardeen Faisal, a games programmer in my third year studying at Ontario Tech University in the game Development and Entrepreneurship program. I entered a game development program because I want to create wonderful games like the ones I grew up playing. Games have always been a large part of my life. Playing games with my friends, family, and just all by myself. I love the experience developers pour their souls into to create and deliver to their audiences. When I hit the age of deciding what career I wanted to pursue, the first thing that came to my mind was developing games. I love seeing the process of how teams of people come together across all different fields like art, design, and programming to create one fully functioning game.

When I entered my second year of university I found that programming was my passion. Although any programmer will tell you that programming can be (and will be) frustrating half the time, the joy I feel when I solve problems with my code or program a new mechanic/feature is unparalelled. Having the power to think of a game idea and actually make it into a reality is something really special. I hope to grow as a programmer and a general game developer to produce bigger and better games.

Thanks for taking the time to read up about me!
